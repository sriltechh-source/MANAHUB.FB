Terms & Conditions

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using Mana Hub, you agree to these terms. We provide local delivery and services in the Nirmal district area.

2. Services

Mana Hub connects customers with local delivery partners, service providers, vendors and businesses. We act as an intermediary platform.

3. Orders & Payments

All orders are placed via WhatsApp. Pricing is subject to change based on market rates. Cash on delivery is the primary payment method unless otherwise specified.

4. Delivery Policy

Delivery times vary based on location and availability. We cover Khanapur, Bhainsa, Nirmal, and surrounding areas in the Nirmal district.

5. Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch by contacting us on WhatsApp. Once dispatched, cancellations may not be possible.

6. Privacy

We collect location data only for delivery purposes and with your consent. Your data is not sold to third parties.

7. Location Data

GPS location is captured and shared with your order for accurate delivery. You may opt out, but this may affect service quality.

8. Liability

Mana Hub is not liable for delays caused by weather, traffic, or other uncontrollable factors. We strive to provide the best service possible.

9. Changes

We may update these terms periodically. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact

For any issues: WhatsApp 7799086877 or 7993477015

Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect your name, phone number, address, and GPS location when you use our services or fill out booking forms.

How We Use It

Your information is used solely to process orders, provide delivery services, and improve our platform. GPS data is included in WhatsApp order messages for accurate delivery.

Data Storage

Profile data is stored locally on your device using browser storage. We do not maintain a central database of user information.

Notifications

With your consent, we send push notifications about orders and offers via OneSignal. You can opt out at any time.

Third Parties

We use Microsoft Clarity for analytics and WhatsApp for communication. We do not sell your data to any third parties.

Your Rights

You can clear your saved profile anytime from the More page. You can revoke notification permissions from your browser settings.

Help & FAQ
How do I place an order?
Browse our Store or Services, add items, fill in your details and tap "Place Order via WhatsApp". Our team will confirm your order within minutes.
What areas do you deliver to?
We cover Khanapur, Bhainsa, Mudhole, Sarangapur, Nirmal, Dilawarpur, Mamda, Kubeer, Narsapur, Kaddam, Jukkal and surrounding areas in Nirmal district.
What is Dabbawala Tiffin Service?
Our Dabbawala service delivers fresh home-cooked tiffin meals to your doorstep daily. You can subscribe for weekly or monthly plans at discounted rates.
How is my location used?
Your GPS location is automatically captured and included in your order message to help our delivery team find you accurately. It is never stored on our servers.
Can I cancel my order?
Yes! Contact us on WhatsApp before the order is dispatched. Once dispatched, cancellation may not be possible but we'll try our best to help.
What payment methods are accepted?
We primarily accept Cash on Delivery (COD). UPI payments can also be arranged — ask our team on WhatsApp.
How does Refer & Earn work?
Share your unique referral code with friends. When they place their first order using your code, both of you get a discount on your next order!
